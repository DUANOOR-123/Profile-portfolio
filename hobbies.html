SOMETHING ABOUT MY HOBBIES

There has never been a year in my life when I could stay consistently committed to one thing. My hobbies tend to change from day to day. Sometimes, I find myself spending endless hours immersed in developing new projects and brainstorming fresh ideas. Other times, I crave the simplicity of rest—just lying in bed, enjoying the comfort of a cozy environment. There are also moments when I embrace my "girl era," where I find joy in cleaning, cooking, and organizing. My days unfold in a way that I believe is planned by ALLAH. I don’t often make plans unless there’s a deadline to meet. In short, I’m a bit moody, and as I always say, I do what I love.
